Who we are

We are a community of riders. Founded in 2018 in Harrisonburg, Virginia we founded Shenandoah Cycling Club for all skill levels, from the pro rider to the rider who is brand new. Dedicated to always including everyone who has even the slightest passion for the sport. After a few years of having the club we have expanded to having weekly rides, hosting races, and even throwing club parties. Shenandoah Cycling Club is always expanding and even offers road bikes and mountain bikes for rent if you need a set of wheels.

We are excited and ambitous for growing our club in the next year to having multiple sites outside of Harrisonburg including Charlottesville and Front Royal. If you are interested in getting more information or joining the Shenandoah Cycling Club click on over to our become a member tab.
